## Micromechanics of frictional sliding
Fault frictional stability is usually described in the context of slip weakening or rate-state friction. However, these description do not fully capture the complex behaviors at the nano- to microscope asperity scale elastic and plastic deformations. I perform continuous ultrasonic monitoring and study the temporal evolution of elastic wave amplitudes and velocities to better understand the microscopic origins of fault friction.
